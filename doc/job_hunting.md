## 4. Featured Projects (Use for "Featured" Section or Posts)

### **Interactive Workbook: The Silent Crisis**
*Education Tech*
Traditional workbooks are silent. I built a bilingual learning platform that gives students a "safe space to stutter" before they have to speak in class.
*   **Tech:** React, Firebase, Tailwind
*   **Key Feature:** "Sing Along" mode to bypass anxiety centers in the brain.

### **Price Lock: An Anxiety Medication**
*Fintech / Travel*
Dynamic pricing makes users feel helpless. "Price Lock" is a button that pauses inflation for 24 hours, giving travelers peace of mind while they wait for approval.
*   **Impact:** Aims to convert "browsing anxiety" into "secured intent".

### **Project Kinship: Digital Warmth**
*Social Logistics*
Remittance is usually cold and numeric. Kinship allows the diaspora to send "Care Packages" (food/goods) instead of just cash, bridging the emotional distance with logistics.

### **FilterMe: The Mirror Test**
*AR Commerce experiment*
An AR experiment to solve the "Realness Gap" in online shopping. If a user can "wear" a product via a filter, they trust it.
*   **Learning:** High engagement (Fun) does not always equal high trust (Conversion).
